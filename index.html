Google Music for Mac

Wrapper for Google Music that turns All Access into a separate app that is compatible with the Mac's media keys.

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub
Media keys

Control your music without having to switch applications. Google Music for Mac responds to the media keys built into your computer, allowing you to easily control the playback of your music from anywhere.

Notifications

Get notifications whenever the currently playing song changes.

Adjustable appearance

Use the built-in appearance to make Google Music for Mac look like a beautiful application, or stick with Google's original theme.
